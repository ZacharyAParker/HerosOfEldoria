# Asura — 5e Homebrew Race

*Guild Wars 2 inspired. World lore lives in WORLD_BIBLE.md (Peoples of Aeridor — the asura
surfaced from the Undervaults, organize into krewes, and are currently insured as "wagons
(self-moving)").*

## Traits

- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Intelligence, +1 to one other ability of your choice
- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 25 ft.
- **Age:** Mature at 10, live up to 80 years
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft. (subterranean origin)
- **Magical Affinity:** You know the *mage hand* cantrip. At 3rd level, you can cast *arcane
  lock* once per long rest (no material component cost). Intelligence is your spellcasting
  ability for these.
- **Tinker's Ingenuity:** You have proficiency with tinker's tools. If you already have that
  proficiency, double your proficiency bonus for checks made with them instead.
- **Krewe Tactics:** Once per short rest, when you or a creature within 10 ft. of you makes an
  ability check, you can grant advantage on it as a reaction (representing krewe-style teamwork).
- **Languages:** Common, plus one of your choice

## Designer's notes

Mage hand and arcane lock are intentionally light spellcasting — enough flavor for an inventor
race without stepping on the Artificer class's own spells and infusions.

If a mechanically fixed ASI is preferred over the floating +1, flat +2 Intelligence /
+1 Constitution works well for a durable little inventor.

Flavor: asura are short, brilliant subterranean inventors and mages who value cleverness above
all else. They organize into small task forces called krewes rather than formal governments, and
are known for magitech constructs and golemancy.
